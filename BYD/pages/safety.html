Safety and responsibility notes

This repository should be treated as a convenience directory, not as a guarantee that an APK is safe, legal or compatible.

Only use apps while parked unless the function is clearly intended for driving, such as navigation voice guidance.
Verify APK hashes and source links before redistributing files.
Keep a known good copy of every APK before replacing it with a newer version.
Avoid granting unnecessary permissions, especially accessibility, notifications, contacts, SMS and location.
Do not use sensitive accounts on modified apps unless you fully accept the risk.

Some third party apps may breach platform terms, local rules or vehicle warranty expectations. Make an informed decision before installing.
